The 21 Point
Authority
Diagnostic

Score yourself honestly across the four pillars of total authority. Most men overestimate two and are blind to the other two. The score is not the point. The pattern is.
"Competence without the signals to match it
is authority that never lands."

Before you begin
Answer as you are, not as you intend to be.
The gap between those two things is precisely what this diagnostic is designed to find.
Score each statement from 1 to 5 using the scale on the cover. Total your score for each pillar separately, then combine for your overall reading. A low score on any single pillar reveals where your authority is leaking most — regardless of how well the other three are performing.

Reading your score

You have strong pillars and weak ones. The room reads the weak ones regardless of how well the others perform. Your lowest pillar score is your most important number.

Your signals are working against your competence in most rooms. The gap between how capable you are and how you are being read is costing you significantly.

The room is not reading you as the most capable person in it regardless of whether you are. Every pillar requires deliberate reconstruction. This is where the work begins.
Note the pillar where your score is lowest. That is not a weakness of character. It is a signal failure. Specific, identifiable, and correctable with precision. The overall score tells you where you stand. The pillar breakdown tells you exactly where to begin.
